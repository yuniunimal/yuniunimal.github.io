    If u want to visit the beautiful place in lhokseumawe city there is some place Goa Jepang, Jangka beach, Ujoeng Blang Beach, Waduk Jeulikat Japanese Guha Bouquet this place is a Japanese heritage cave on a hill. A historic site, always gives its own imagination for us to guess what has happened and what place it was in the past. To get the story, it's incomplete if you don't visit directly to this Japanese Guha Bouquet. Seumadu Island is famous as a tourist attraction of choice in the city of Lhokseumawe. Riyadhah Park is the only city park in Lhokseumawe City. The atmosphere of the place is cool, comfortable and coupled with the internet connection "Free Wifi" in this park, making everyone who visits do not want to leave this park.
